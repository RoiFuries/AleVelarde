About &
Background

I’m Ale Velarde, a Hair and Makeup Designer and Celebrity Men's Groomer, driven by a passion that has shaped my successful career in film and advertising. Throughout my journey, I’ve had the honor of collaborating with prominent celebrities. My dedication and love for this craft have earned me two Ariel Awards.
